Our Team

Monokul originally started as a research project, but our personal investment in the problem space has led it to evolve to its current state: a custom-engineered tool to help you identify problems that we've think could be more effectively solved with access to data.

As a small, flexible team, we're passionate about working closely with you to create a product that seamlessly integrates itself into your organization.
